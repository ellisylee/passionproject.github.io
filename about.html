About me

Hi! I am Ellis Lee, a self-motivated designer, enthusiastic powerlifter, frequent doodler, inquisitive researcher, and decent cook. As a Cognitive Science major, specializing in Human Computer Interaction, I especially enjoy understanding user behavior and utilizing that information to design delightful experiences.

I've worked all sorts of odd jobs like fast food employee, nanny, house keeper, tour guide, and research assistant, to name a few. I even complete few MindSumos to practice problem solving skills in my free time! I just love to work, no matter what it is, and I believe each of my experiences have given me a unique background that translates into my understanding and love of people.
