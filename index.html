Ellis Lee's Passion Project 2016

Triteia Indiegogo Campaign

First Student Organization to Orbit the Moon

SEDS UCSD's cube satellite, Triteia, is competing to fly on NASA’s most powerful rocket ever to be ejected into lunar orbit as part of their mission. It will be: the first cube sat with a chemical propulsion system, the first satellite propelled by a 3D printed engine to orbit the moon, and among the first cube sats to leave low Earth orbit. To support their campaign, I made the graphics for the site.
